Worship Services

Sundays at Evangel Church are set aside for corporate worship at 10:00 AM Sundays. Our worship service is designed to glorify God and encourage the church body. It always includes singing of worshipful songs (modern and occasional hymns), prayer, and a sermon that explains passages from the Bible. As we gather we hope to exalt God together, know Him more intimately, love Him more, and be better equipped to serve Him daily.

Pastor Jeff Heidt and Karlie Huff-Heidt

Pastor Jeff Heidt has been the Pastor of Evangel Church since 2009. His wife is Karlie Huff-Heidt and has five children, Drew Heidt, Olivia Lupo, Clayton Heidt, Tessa Heidt, and Lily Huff. He graduated at Zion Bible Institute and has been pastoring since graduation.
